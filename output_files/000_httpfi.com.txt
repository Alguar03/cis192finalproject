    <div id="mainContent" class="mainContent" ><table id="ChannelTable"><tr><td><div itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Article"><table id="ArticleTable"><tr><td><span itemprop="name"><h1>Fisher Investments Education Center for Individual Investors</h1></span></td></tr><tr><td><span itemprop="articleBody"><p>
 Education is a key part of <a href="http://www.fi.com/about-fisher-investments.aspx" target="_self">Fisher Investments&#39;</a> high-touch service model for individual investors. Whether you enjoy learning about the markets and discussing investing details or prefer to receive high-level executive summaries of the key factors impacting your investments, we can accommodate your individual needs.</p>
<p>
 &nbsp;</p>
<p>
 The firm offers several online resources for individuals with daily commentary on the markets and investing. <a href="http://www.marketminder.com/" target="_blank">Fisher Investments MarketMinder</a> brings readers the most pertinent and influential news of the day, along with insightful research analysis and market commentary. Additional commentary is available on&nbsp;Fisher Investments&rsquo; blogs, <a href="http://fisher-investments.typepad.com/" target="_blank">Global Market Perspective</a> and <a href="http://investingiq.wordpress.com/" target="_blank">Investing IQ</a>.</p>
<p>
 &nbsp;</p>
<p>
 In partnership with John Wiley & Sons, the firm publishes a series of investment books under the <a href="http://fisher-investments-press.wiley.com/" target="_blank">Fisher Investments Press</a>&nbsp;imprint. These books cover topics ranging from behavioral finance to global investing to wealth building. Fisher Investments profiles the primary financial sectors in the <em>Fisher Investments On series</em> including <a href="http://www.prweb.com/releases/health-care/Fisher-Invesments/prweb5153614.htm" target="_blank">Fisher Investments on Health Care</a>, <a href="http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/announcing-fisher-investments-on-technology-116223949.html" target="_blank">Fisher Investments on Technology</a>, and others.</p>
<p>
 &nbsp;</p>
<p>
 CEO Ken Fisher has been writing the monthly <a href="http://search.forbes.com/search/colArchiveSearch?author=Fisher" target="_blank"><em>Forbes</em> Portfolio Strategy column</a> since 1984 and has written several <a href="http://www.ken-fisher-investments.com/author/default.aspx" target="_blank">bestselling books</a> including <em>The Only Three Questions that Count</em>, <em>The Ten Roads to Riches</em>, <em>How to Smell a Rat</em>, and <em>Debunkery</em>.</p>
