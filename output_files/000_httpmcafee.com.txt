      <p>McAfee is the world’s largest dedicated security technology  company. Delivering proactive and proven solutions and services that help  secure systems and networks around the world, McAfee protects consumers and  businesses of all sizes from the latest malware and emerging online threats. Our  solutions are designed to work together, integrating antimalware,  antispyware, and antivirus software with security management features that  deliver unsurpassed real-time visibility and analytics, reduce risk, ensure  compliance, improve Internet security, and help businesses achieve operational efficiencies.</p>
      <p>Backed by an award-winning research team, McAfee security technologies  use a unique, predictive capability that is powered by McAfee Global Threat  Intelligence — enabling home users and businesses to stay one step ahead of  online threats.</p>
      <p>McAfee’s security products and solutions span the following areas:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="/us/products/data-protection/index.aspx">Data  Protection</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/database-security/index.aspx">Database Security</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/email-and-web-security/index.aspx">Email  &amp; Web Security</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/endpoint-protection/index.aspx">Endpoint  Protection</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/mobile-security/index.aspx">Mobile Security</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/network-security/index.aspx">Network  Security</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/risk-and-compliance/index.aspx">Risk  &amp; Compliance</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/security-as-a-service/index.aspx">Security-as-a-Service  (Security SaaS)</a></li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/security-management/index.aspx">Security  Management</a></li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>For Consumers</strong><br />
      McAfee  enables users to safely connect to the Internet, and securely surf and shop the  web. With <a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/all-access-security">McAfee All Access</a>, <a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/total-protection">McAfee Total  Protection</a>, <a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/internet-security">McAfee  Internet Security</a>, and <a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/antivirus-plus">McAfee  AntiVirus Plus</a>, consumers get complete antimalware and antispyware  protection, along with an integrated firewall to ensure their computer systems  remain virus-free. Additionally, McAfee’s <a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/virus-removal-service">virus removal service</a> helps you easily clean viruses and spyware from your PC — all from the comfort of your home. Try out McAfee software by downloading our <a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/free-antivirus-trials">free antivirus</a> trials.</p>
      <p><a href="http://home.mcafee.com/store/mobile-security">McAfee Mobile Security</a> provides comprehensive mobile device protection with antivirus, anti-theft, and web and app protection for smartphones and Android tablets. Get a <a href="https://www.mcafeemobilesecurity.com/">free trial of McAfee Mobile Security</a>.
      </p>
      <p><strong>For Businesses</strong><br />
      McAfee solutions deliver the highest levels of threat  visibility and antimalware protection, including comprehensive system and endpoint protection, network security, cloud security, database security, and data protection. McAfee’s complete security solutions  extend beyond virus software. Backed by McAfee Global Threat Intelligence, our solutions  help companies enhance visibility into their security postures, allowing business  to embrace Web 2.0 technology, virtualization, cloud computing, and personal  and mobile devices, while protecting critical assets and sensitive data.</p>
      <p>Our industry-leading security offerings include:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="/us/products/total-protection-for-endpoint-enterprise-edition.aspx"><strong>McAfee Total Protection for Endpoint —  Enterprise Edition</strong></a>: A single, integrated solution, complete with a  built-in desktop firewall, which secures systems and data against sophisticated  malware, zero-day attacks, noncompliant systems, and unauthorized devices.</li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/network-security-platform.aspx"><strong>McAfee Network Security Platform</strong></a>: Our  best-in-class intrusion prevention system that delivers real-time defenses  against known, zero-day, denial-of-service (DoS), distributed denial-of-service  (DDoS), SYN flood, and encrypted attacks.</li>
        <li><a href="/us/products/enterprise-mobility-management.aspx"><strong>McAfee Enterprise Mobility Management</strong></a>:  A revolutionary security solution that helps businesses offer employees mobile  device choice — including iPhones, iPads, and Android devices — while providing  secure and easy access to corporate mobile applications.</li>
      </ul>
