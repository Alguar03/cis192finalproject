<h4 style="margin:0;padding:0;">Cloud-based HR Software and Solutions</h4><br />
SilkRoad HR software and solutions focus on delivering the finest employee experience by engaging, developing, and enabling employees so they can reach their full potential and have a positive impact on the organization.
<br/><br/>

<h4 style="margin:0;padding:0;"> What is Social Talent Management?</h4><br />
Talent Management is about driving higher <a href="http://silkroad.com/HR_Software/HeartBeat/Overview.html" target="_blank">employee engagement</a>, promoting career development, and enabling the employee to perform at their highest potential and significantly contribute to your company’s bottom line.  Higher employee engagement starts with providing a positive and personal <a href="http://www.silkroad.com/HR_Software/RedCarpet-Onboarding-Software/Overview.html" target="_blank">onboarding</a> experience by leveraging specific employer-branded recruiting and onboarding approaches to nurture the employee and immerse them in the company culture.   Career development goes beyond employee training and starts by aligning the employee objectives with your corporate goals and creating personalized learning plans to help the employee achieve those goals.  Enabled employees are empowered to drive their career and establish their own destiny by analyzing and understanding the influence and impact they’ve made within the organization. The term "Social Talent Management" acknowledges the higher-level concept that the best talent management solutions are tied to technology -- and in this case, the <a href="http://www.silkroad.com/HR_Software/OpenHire-Applicant-Tracking-Software/Overview.html" target="_blank">Social recruitment technologies</a> that have become so important to the employees of today's workforces.
 <br /><br />
<h4 style="margin:0;padding:0;">What are Talent Management Solutions?</h4><br />
