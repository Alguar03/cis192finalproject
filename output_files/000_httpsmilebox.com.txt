<a href="iphone/index.html"><img src="images/smilebox-mobile.png" width="460" height="289" alt="Smilebox for iPhone" /></a><h2>What is Smilebox?</h2>
<p>At Smilebox we believe that how you share your photos should be as memorable as the photos themselves. So we've designed a new way to bring your photos and videos to life.</p>
<p>              Our simple application for PC or Mac lets you quickly and easily create <a href="slideshows.html">slideshows</a>, <a href="invitations.html">invitations</a>, <a href="greetings.html">greetings</a>, <a href="collages.html">collages</a>, <a href="scrapbooks.html">scrapbooks</a> and <a href="photo-albums.html">photo albums</a> right on your computer. With more than 1000 customizable templates to choose from, you'll find inspiration around every corner. With <a href="iphone/index.html">our new iPhone app</a> you can share your moments on the go as well. </p>
<p>              And Smilebox gives you more ways to share: Facebook, email, blog, printing at home or to a store, and burning to DVD. Smilebox makes sharing photos with your family and friends easy, unique and memorable.<br />
</p>
