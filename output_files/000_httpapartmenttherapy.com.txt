On April 26th, MoMA will launch the tenth edition of its <em>Destination: Design</em> program, featuring contemporary design from Mexico. The selection includes over 150 products including tableware, small furniture items, jewelry and paper goods, that will be available at the MoMA design store for a limited time (April-June, 2012).<br /> <a href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/destination-mexico-at-moma-169412" class="more-link">More</a></p>
